From Harmony School of Enrichment - Houston Every child isn’t the same. So why should every school be?
Harmony School of Enrichment – is a tuition-free, open-enrollment public charter school providing a high-quality education with a focus on Science, Technology, Engineering, Math (STEM) for students in Grades K-5 and offers services to students with specialized needs.
The school is a part of Harmony Public Schools, a nationally-recognized school system that believes each student can achieve their own unique, full potential through a caring collaborative learning environment that emphasizes personalized learning innovative instructional methods.
Our award-winning curriculum prepares students from under served communities across Texas for college success career readiness. Additionally, our nationally-recognized Character Education program teaches students the importance of personal responsibility, self-respect, empathy, valuing others.
